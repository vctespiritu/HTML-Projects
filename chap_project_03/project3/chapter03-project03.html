Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière

by Joan-Auguste-Dominique Ingres

The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière was painted in 1805 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean Auguste Dominique
Ingres, and today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of three portraits of the Rivière family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1805
Medium: oil on canvas
Dimensions: 100 x 70 cm
Home: Muske du Louvre, Paris
